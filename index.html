Let's Eat! User Manual

By: Brett Tomita, Hayden Matheus, Long Chu, Pamela Regudo, and Jessica Hilario

What is Let's Eat?

The purpose of our Let’s Eat! is to help people choose what restaurant they should go to.
It will create a list of restaurants based on the filters the user has put in.
Our application’s features reduces the frustration of finding somewhere to eat, encourages users
to experience somewhere new, and requires less memory space since the directions will pop up on the
application as well. The main elements are the end users and the system because
it is the basis of what our application is trying to accomplish.

Getting Started: Making an Account
  • Select Sign In from the Home Page
  • Select Sign Up
  • Provide your email address, password, and any other requested information
  • Select Submit, then an email will be sent to you to confirm your new account

Getting Started: Finding A Restaurant
  • Select the search bar
  • Enter your preference filters
  • Select Find a Restaurant
  • Restaurant's basic information will be displayed
  • Select View Yelp Page to view restaurant's Yelp page
  • Select Get Directions to get directions

Getting Started: Group Voting
  • Select Group Vote from the Home Page
  • A group invite code will be generated. Share with people to include them in the vote.
    If you are friends with them then select them from the Friends List to send an invite.
  • When code is received, select Join Group from the Group Vote page
  • Once all group members have joined, select Start Group Vote
  • Each member will be prompted to enter their preferred restaurant filters
  • Countdown timer on the vote page indicates time left to apply filters
  • If Find a Restaurant is selected or time expires, all current filters will be taken
  • Chosen restaurant is presented to all group members
  • Each member can choose to view Yelp page or get directions
Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a free service?
There is no charge to use this system. You will not be asked to provide any payment information.

Do you share our data with any businesses?
Privacy is important to us; all of your data is encrypted and stored securely in our system.

Can businesses advertise on the app?
Businesses can choose to advertise their businesses on the app,
however we will not share any of your information with them
